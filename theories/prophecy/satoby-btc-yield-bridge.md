## Core theory

The most internally-consistent reading of Satoby is **not** a flatcoin but a Bitcoin bridge / real-asset swap. The name itself is a Schelling point: **Sato**shi + To**by**. The thesis: Toby + Taboshi + Patience are the inputs; **Satoswap** (the fourth rune, "$sato 🔺") is the AMM/swap layer; and **Satoby** is the output reward — a BTC-denominated yield asset minted when you route the elements through Satoswap. Yield is paid in **cbBTC** (Coinbase's wrapped BTC on Base), not in dollars or USDC.

This makes TobyWorld a real-world-asset (RWA) swap in the literal sense crypto uses the term: it converts a Base-native meme position into a claim on actual Bitcoin. "Wrap any asset with Toby to mint Satoby" (@nn4m3) generalizes it — Toby becomes the gateway asset that any holding can be paired with to produce a BTC-yielding instrument. The Satoshi mythology (Toadgod-as-Satoshi, a dormant ~1M BTC wallet) is the narrative skin over a concrete mechanic: BTC liquidity flowing to time-tested holders.

## Why it may be close

This reading is favored because it requires no new infrastructure that contradicts the lore — it uses cbBTC (which exists on Base), a DEX (Satoswap, named in the roadmap), and a reward token (Satoby, confirmed canon). The naming is too on-the-nose to be accidental: Taboshi and Satoby are anagof Toby+Satoshi, and the whole ecosystem is themed around Bitcoin's founder. The cbBTC-launch timing correlation gives it an external anchor. Multiple independent members converge on "yield paid in BTC, not USD," which is unusual specificity.

---

## Why it may be far off

"TG holds a Satoshi-era 1M BTC wallet and will distribute it" is the load-bearing claim for the grandest version, and it is pure speculation with no on-chain proof — the dormant Satoshi coins have never moved. A more mundane explanation: Toadgod made some cbBTC buys to seed an LP, and the community extrapolated a BTC central bank from it. The "wrap any asset to mint Satoby" generalization is also unconstrained — it could describe almost any DeFi vault. And this thesis directly competes with the flatcoin reading; both cannot be fully true, which means at least one large community cluster is wrong.
