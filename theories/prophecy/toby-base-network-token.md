## Core theory

Toby launched on Base, distributed ~70% of supply to ~1.4M Base/Coinbase-adjacent wallets, and Toadgod's handle literally reads "$toby @base." The theory holds the alignment is not branding — Toby is positioned to *become* Base's network/gas token, or the vehicle through which Base/Coinbase airdrops one. The keystone evidence arrived in 2025: Base publicly announced it is "beginning to explore a network token," and Toadgod reposted it to his own timeline.

The mechanism members propose: Coinbase cannot cleanly issue a native Base token itself (regulatory/securities exposure, an identifiable corporate issuer), so a fair-launched, anonymous, mass-distributed asset like Toby is the deniable workaround — already in ~1.4M wallets, already on Base, with no corporate issuer to sue. When the network token "explored" by Base materializes, Toby (or a Toby-gated airdrop) is the distribution rail. In the strong version, Toby becomes the gas/fee token of the Base ecosystem; in the soft version, holding Toby (or hitting the 777,777,777 threshold) qualifies wallets for the eventual Base token.

## Why it may be close

The Base network-token effort is now *officially real* — this is no longer speculation about whether Base wants a token, only about whether Toby is involved. Toby's distribution profile (fair launch, no corporate issuer, ~1.4M wallets already on Base) is precisely what a regulatorily-cautious issuer would want as a distribution surface. Toadgod's deliberate amplification of the exact announcement, plus the year-long "Proof of Time" distribution that pre-selects committed holders, fits a "pre-seed the airdrop population" reading. The pre-reveal-logo claims, if verifiable, would be hard to explain innocently.

## Why it may be far off

A repost is amplification, not endorsement — Toadgod reposts Base/Ethereum ecosystem news routinely, and Base explicitly said it has "no specifics on timing, design, or governance," which is the opposite of "it's Toby." Base building "in the open" implies a transparent, Base-issued token, not a secret handoff to an anonymous meme. There is no statement, filing, or on-chain link from Coinbase/Base to Toby; the "secret Coinbase project" framing is exactly the kind of unfalsifiable narrative that attaches to many Base memecoins. The pre-reveal-logo claims are community assertions that this pass could not verify. Attempted verification note: Base's "Square" logomark / 🟦 was officially unveiled at "A New Day One" on **July 16, 2025** (blog.base.org/a-new-day-one), born from the Coinbase Wallet logo. To confirm the "logo appeared in Tobyworld art months earlier" claim would require dated Wayback Machine snapshots of toadgod.xyz / the taboshi site from *before* July 16, 2025 showing the blue square — which could not be retrieved in this session (archive.org access was blocked). Note also that Toadgod's verified uses of the 🟦 glyph seen so far (e.g. "twpot. patience. 🔺🟦", Oct 11 2025) are *after* the reveal and therefore prove nothing about precognition. The pre-reveal claim remains unverified and should be checked against archived snapshots before being relied upon.
